Tardigrades (moss piglets)

Tardigrades are one of the most remarkable animals on the Earth. They able to survive in extreme environments that would kill almost any other animal.

  • Habitat: Everywhere even space (10 days)
  • The highest temperature: 151 °C
  • The lowest temperature: −272 °C
  • Dehydration: can survive in a dry state 10 years

Most range from 0.3 to 0.5 mm in length, although the largest species may reach 1.2 mm. The body consists of a head, three body segments with a pair of legs each, and a caudal segment with a fourth pair of legs.
